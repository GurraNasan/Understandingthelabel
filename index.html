General fact

France has always been considered the homeland of quality wines, and the originals of almost all classic wines is assumed to come from France. But they are also a big producer of quantity wines. Next after Italy, they are the most wine producing country in the world. With around 46,7 milion hector liter each year. So how can we know what wine that is quality wine or quantity wine? A smart way is to undertand the label on the bottle and the wine classification in France.

Speed fact

  • AOP/AOC: Appellation di Origine Protégée/Contrôlée
  • IGP: Indication Géographique Protégée
  • VdF: Vin de France
  • AOP/AOC: Small areas with tightly defined regulations
  • IGP: Lager areas with fewer regulations
  • VdF: Wine with out a geographical indication
