Alsace

White wine is the dominating wine sort that is made in Alsace.
The allowed grapes are Riesling, Pinot gris, Gewurztraminer and muscat for white wine and Pinot noir for red wine.
So how can we know what wine that is quality wine or quantity wine? A smart way is to undertand the label on the bottle and the wine classification in France.


Speed fact

  • Green grapes:Riesling, Pinot gris, Gewurztraminer and muscat
  • Blue grapes: Pinot Noir
  • Maturation: Steel tank or old oak barrels for white wine, New oak for red wine

Burgundy

Speed fact

  • Green grapes:Riesling, Pinot gris, Gewurztraminer and muscat
  • Blue grapes: Pinot Noir
  • Maturation: Steel tank or old oak barrels for white wine, New oak for red wine
  • AOP/AOC: Small areas with tightly defined regulations
  • IGP: Lager areas with fewer regulations
  • VdF: Wine with out a geographical indication
